# ATHLYNX Privacy Policy
## Effective Date: January 22, 2026

**ATHLYNX Corporation** ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile applications and services.

---

## 1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

### 1.1 Personal Information
We collect information that you provide directly to us:

- **Account Information:** Name, email address, phone number, date of birth, profile picture
- **Athlete Profile:** Sport(s), position, statistics, achievements, school/team affiliations
- **Identity Verification:** Government ID (for age verification and compliance)
- **Payment Information:** Billing address, payment method details (processed by Stripe)

### 1.2 Health and Fitness Data
Certain apps collect health-related information:

- **Diamond Grind:** Workout logs, exercise data, body measurements, progress photos, nutrition logs
- **Faith:** Wellness assessments, mental health check-ins (anonymized)
- **Portal:** Optional health data for athlete profiles

**HIPAA Compliance:** We maintain HIPAA-compliant practices for health data storage and transmission. Health data is encrypted at rest and in transit.

### 1.3 Usage Data
We automatically collect certain information:

- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Information:** App features used, time spent, navigation patterns
- **Location Data:** Approximate location (city/state) for content personalization
- **Communications:** Messages sent through our messaging services (encrypted)

### 1.4 Content You Create
- Posts and comments on social feeds
- Photos and videos uploaded
- Playbooks and training plans
- NIL deal information (stored securely)

## 3. HIPAA COMPLIANCE STATEMENT

### 3.1 Our Commitment
ATHLYNX maintains HIPAA-compliant practices for all health-related data:

- **Encryption:** All health data encrypted using AES-256 encryption
- **Access Controls:** Strict role-based access to health information
- **Audit Logs:** All access to health data is logged and monitored
- **Business Associate Agreements:** We maintain BAAs with all service providers handling health data
- **Training:** Staff trained on HIPAA compliance annually

### 3.2 Protected Health Information (PHI)
The following data is treated as PHI:
- Workout and training data (Diamond Grind)
- Injury information
- Body measurements and health metrics
- Wellness and mental health data (Faith)

### 3.3 Your Rights Under HIPAA
You have the right to:
- Access your health information
- Request corrections to your health data
- Receive an accounting of disclosures
- Request restrictions on use of your health data
- Receive communications confidentially

**To exercise these rights:** Email privacy@athlynx.ai

## 4. DATA SECURITY MEASURES

We implement industry-standard security measures:

### 4.1 Technical Safeguards
- **Encryption in Transit:** TLS 1.3 for all data transmission
- **Encryption at Rest:** AES-256 encryption for stored data
- **Secure Authentication:** Multi-factor authentication available
- **Regular Security Audits:** Quarterly penetration testing
- **Vulnerability Scanning:** Continuous monitoring for threats

### 4.2 Organizational Safeguards
- Background checks for employees with data access
- Confidentiality agreements for all personnel
- Regular security training
- Incident response plan and procedures

### 4.3 Physical Safeguards
- Secure data centers with SOC 2 Type II certification
- Controlled access to server infrastructure
- Environmental controls and monitoring

## 5. INFORMATION SHARING AND DISCLOSURE

### 5.1 We DO NOT Sell Your Data
We do not and will not sell your personal information to third parties.

### 5.2 Service Providers
We share data with trusted service providers:

- **Cloud Hosting:** AWS (SOC 2, HIPAA compliant)
- **Payment Processing:** Stripe (PCI DSS compliant)
- **Email Services:** AWS SES
- **SMS Services:** AWS SNS
- **Analytics:** Privacy-focused analytics (anonymized data only)

All service providers sign Data Processing Agreements (DPAs) and Business Associate Agreements (BAAs) where applicable.

### 5.3 With Your Consent
We may share information when you explicitly consent:

- Connecting with coaches or recruiters
- Sharing profile with colleges (Transfer Portal)
- Collaborating on training plans
- Brand partnership opportunities (NIL Vault)

### 5.4 Legal Requirements
We may disclose information to:

- Comply with legal process (subpoena, court order)
- Enforce our Terms of Service
- Protect rights and safety
- Prevent fraud or security issues

### 5.5 Business Transfers
In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred. We will notify you via email and app notification.
