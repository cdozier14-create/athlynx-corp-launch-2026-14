# ATHLYNX AI Launches Revolutionary Athlete Success Platform
## Mississippi-Based Startup Aims to Democratize NIL Opportunities for All Athletes

**HUMBLE, TX** — Dozier Holdings Group today announced the official launch of ATHLYNX AI, a comprehensive athlete success platform designed to democratize Name, Image, and Likeness (NIL) opportunities for athletes at every level.

The platform, which went live at 9:00 AM CST, features 10 specialized applications, 8 AI-powered advisor "wizards," and a revolutionary Transfer Portal that provides real-time NIL valuations for college athletes.

"We believe everyone is an NIL commodity," said Chad A. Dozier Sr., Founder and CEO of ATHLYNX AI Corporation. "Whether you're a high school athlete dreaming of going pro, a college player in the transfer portal, a veteran transitioning to civilian life, or a blue-collar worker building your personal brand—ATHLYNX is for you."

### Key Platform Features:

**Transfer Portal**
- Position-based search functionality for coaches and recruiters
- Real-time NIL valuations for every listed player
- Star ratings and social media metrics
- Direct contact information for streamlined recruiting

**AI Wizard Advisors**
- Agent Wizard: Finding and vetting sports agents
- Lawyer Bot: Contract review and legal guidance
- Financial Advisor AI: Money management and tax planning
- Scout Simulator: Understanding what scouts look for
- Scholarship Navigator: NCAA rules and eligibility
- Transfer Guide: Step-by-step portal process
- Life Coach AI: Balancing athletics and academics
- Career Transition Planner: Post-college preparation

**10 Specialized Apps**
The platform includes dedicated applications for training tracking, game strategy, NIL deal management, content creation, and more.
