## Non-Negotiable Acquisition Terms

### 1. Equity Retention
**Minimum:** 5% equity in acquiring company or resulting entity
**Preferred:** 10-15% equity with anti-dilution protection
**Vesting:** Immediate vesting of all founder shares upon acquisition

### 2. Board Representation
**Requirement:** Guaranteed board seat for minimum 5 years post-acquisition
**Preferred:** Board seat for life or until voluntary resignation
**Voting Rights:** Full voting rights on all major corporate decisions

### 3. Cash Component
**Minimum:** 50% of acquisition price in cash at closing
**Preferred:** 60-70% cash, remainder in acquirer equity
**Escrow:** Maximum 10% holdback for 12 months

### 4. Founder Role Options
Choose one of the following post-acquisition:
- **Executive Role:** CEO, President, or Division Head (3-year minimum term)
- **Advisory Role:** Strategic Advisor with compensation (5-year term)
- **Emeritus Role:** Founder Emeritus title with board seat (lifetime)
- **Clean Exit:** Full cash-out with consulting agreement (2-year term)

### 5. Employee Protection
- All current employees offered positions with acquiring company
- No layoffs for 12 months post-acquisition
- Retention bonuses for key personnel

### 6. Brand Preservation
- "ATHLYNX" brand maintained for minimum 3 years
- "Founded by Chad A. Dozier" recognition preserved
- DHG Trust legacy acknowledged in company history
