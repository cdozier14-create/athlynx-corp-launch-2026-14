## 2. TRANSFER PORTAL WINDOWS (2025-26)

### Key Changes for 2025-26:
- NCAA eliminated spring transfer portal
- Only **Winter Transfer Window** remains
- **Football Portal Opens:** January 2, 2026 (after CFP quarterfinals)
- **Duration:** 2-week frenzied period
- **Coach Departure Rule:** 15-day window starting 5 days after new coach hired
- **30-Day Rule:** Athletes have 30 days from coach departure to enter portal
